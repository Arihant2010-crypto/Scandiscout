Significant Cultural & Architectural Features

1. Nyhavn Waterfront Canal

Cultural Significance: 17th-century heritage commercial port district lined with brightly colored townhouses.

Public Transport Links: Accessible via Kongens Nytorv Metro Station (M1/M2/M3/M4 lines).

2. Christiansborg Palace

Cultural Significance: Government palace hosting the Danish Parliament, Supreme Court, and Ministry of State.

Public Transport Links: Short walk from Gammel Strand Metro Station or Bus line 2A.

3. The Round Tower (Rundetårn)

Cultural Significance: 17th-century astronomical observatory tower built under King Christian IV featuring a unique equestrian helical corridor.

Public Transport Links: Located 200m from Nørreport Station (S-train, Metro, and regional rail link hubs).
